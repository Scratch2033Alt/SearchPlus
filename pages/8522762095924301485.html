Metadata

Paola Di Maio | Posted on: July 6, 2018

AI MetAdata set to describe the AI domain in its various facets  is one of the ultimate outcomes of this group.

We can however start jotting down words and memes to help us think. If you enter a term/category here for consideration (candidate term) please also enter the justfiction/method uses and the knowledge reference/source (where does the term come from)

For example:

WORD                         FUNCTION                         SOURCE/REF           METHOD
