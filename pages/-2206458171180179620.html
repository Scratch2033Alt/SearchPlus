W3C Donations Program

Your donation directly contributes to making the Web work, for everyone: a Web that reflects the principles of accessibility, internationalization, privacy and security which guide our technical work and community engagement.

The support from generous individuals and organizations such as you, who share our values, make a huge difference to our operations as a public-interest non-profit, and help us to achieve our vision.

Other ways to sponsor W3C

W3C recognizes that giving takes many forms. As well as one-time donations we welcome enquiries to discuss how our work aligns with the aspirations and objectives described in the giving portfolios of individuals and family offices, philanthropic organizations and foundations, corporate social responsibility programs, government programs and others.

W3C is also registered with benevity, so that employees of organizations worldwide with structured giving and volunteering programs can get involved, help drive awareness about the importance of W3C work and make a tangible positive contribution to our work.

Please help us to inspire others to do the same, by sharing with your network of contacts.
